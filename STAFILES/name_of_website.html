STA Overview

STA College is a premier educational institution in the Philippines, specializing in Information Technology, Business, Engineering, Hospitality, and Arts & Sciences. Established in 1983, STA has grown into a nationwide network of campuses dedicated to real-life education through industry-driven curriculum and hands-on training.
